Interesting Fact about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles which are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin colours is an important part of communications among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have prehensive tails that they use to rap around tree branches

Interesting Fact about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Fact about Camels

  • Camels are renowned as the name "The Ship Of the Desert"
  • Camels are known for their incredible abilities of surviving without water for 8 to 10 days
  • Most of the camels have two humps but there are some exceptions

Interesting Fact about Teddy Bears

  • Teddy bears are surprisingly small children's favourite toys
  • They are used for cuddling and playing with by kids
  • They come in different shapes and sizes and they can also come in different animal forms

Interesting Fact about Pandas

  • Pandas are BIG eaters – every day they fill their tummies for up to 12 hours
  • Giant pandas (often referred to as simply “pandas”) are black and white bears.
  • Panda Cubs are born blind, but sadly survival rate is low.

Interesting Fact about Flying Cicadas

  • There are about 3,000 known species of cicadas worldwide.
  • A cicada’s body color can be black, brown and green.
  • A cicada’s eye color can be blue, red or white.
